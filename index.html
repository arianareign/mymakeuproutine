Mother vs Daughter Makeup Routine

Ariana: Tell me about your makeup routine.

Avissa: I wash my face, apply moisturizer that my dermatologist recommended, then put on foundation. After that, I put on setting powder. Next, I apply eyeshadow. Then I put on eyebrow gel, mascara, eyeliner, and then highlighter. Next I apply blush and lipstick. Finally, I use setting spray.

Yasaman: I wash my face. I use moisturizer. I put foundation on, apply blush, fill my eyebrows, and apply eyeliner.

Ariana: Avissa, what are the current makeup trends?

Avissa: People are doing designs now, such as clown makeup and butterflies. A very popular trend is the trend of recreating filters from Snapchat and Instagram with makeup.

Ariana: Mom, how has your makeup routine changed over the years?

Yasaman: I am moisturizing more, a lot more.
